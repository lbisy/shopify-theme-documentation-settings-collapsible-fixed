# About and Contact Pages

The theme provides dedicated **About** and **Contact** page templates. Use About to tell the brand story and Contact to display a form and collect customer inquiries.

![About and Contact templates](/images/en/about-contact.svg)

## Create and assign the templates

1. In Shopify admin, go to **Online Store > Pages**.
2. Create separate About and Contact pages.
3. Under **Theme template**, assign the About template to the About page and the Contact template to the Contact page.
4. Save each page and make it visible.
5. Under **Content > Menus** (or **Online Store > Navigation**), add both pages to a header or footer menu.
6. Open **Online Store > Themes > Customize** and use the page selector to edit each page.

If a page does not appear in the selector, confirm that it is saved and uses a template supplied by the current theme.

## About page

The About template can begin with an **About Us** hero and continue with Philosophy Section, Promise Section, Brand Statement, testimonials, before-and-after content, and newsletter sections.

### About Us settings

- **Color scheme**: background, text, and supporting colors.
- **Image**: brand, product-lifestyle, or decorative image.
- **Eyebrow text**: short text above the heading, such as `About Esen`.
- **Subtitle size**: eyebrow or supporting-title size.
- **Heading** and **Heading size**: the main brand statement and its size.
- **Description**: supporting rich text.
- **Image alt text**: an accessible description of the image.
- **Padding Top / Bottom**: vertical spacing in `px`.

See [About Stats](/en/sections/about-stats) for related field, image, and content guidance.

### Recommended page flow

Arrange content as: brand proposition → philosophy → promises → social proof → newsletter prompt. Keep the hero focused on one central idea and place supporting details farther down the page.

## Contact page

The **Contact Us** section places an image beside a form. The form can include First name, Last name, Email, Phone, Message, and a submit button. On mobile, the columns normally stack vertically.

### Colors and form appearance

- **Color scheme**: the section background and surrounding text.
- **Edit scheme**: opens global color settings and can affect other sections using the same scheme.
- **Form background color**
- **Form text color**

Maintain adequate contrast and check input borders, placeholders, and the submit button after changing colors.

### Image

Use **Image** for a brand, store, team, or decorative photo. Add meaningful **Image alt text**, such as `Contact us`. Prefer portrait or near-square compositions with important subjects near the center so responsive cropping does not remove them.

### Heading and description

- **Heading**: the form title, such as `Reach Us For Radiance`.
- **Heading size**
- **Description**: short rich text explaining suitable inquiries or what happens after submission.

Keep the explanation direct and avoid promising response times the team cannot consistently meet.

### Submission feedback and watermark

- **Button text**: for example, `Send`.
- **Success message**: confirm that the form was submitted.
- **Watermark text**: a short decorative brand name such as `Esen`.

Ensure the watermark does not cover the form, privacy links, or other important content.

### Padding

Use **Padding Top** and **Padding Bottom** to control spacing around the section. `32 px / 32 px` is a practical starting point.

## Example

| Setting | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Form background / text | `#FFFFFF` / `#1A1A1A` |
| Heading | `Reach Us For Radiance` |
| Heading size | `XL` |
| Button text | `Send` |
| Success message | `Thank you for your message. We will get back to you soon.` |
| Watermark text | `Esen` |
| Padding | `32 px / 32 px` |

Replace all sample copy with actual brand content before publishing.

## Test the form

1. Check field layout on desktop and mobile.
2. Enter test data and submit.
3. Verify required-field validation, errors, and the success message.
4. Confirm the store receives or records the inquiry.
5. Check CAPTCHA, privacy-policy, and terms links.

Do not leave real customer information or unrelated test content in the live page.

## Before publishing

- Confirm both pages use the correct templates and appear in navigation.
- Replace sample headings, descriptions, buttons, and messages.
- Check image cropping and alt text.
- Review contrast, heading wrapping, form width, and watermark position.
- Preview desktop and mobile and submit one final test form.
- Save the pages, menus, and theme changes.
