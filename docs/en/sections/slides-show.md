# Slideshow

The slideshow presents brand imagery, promotional copy, and calls to action. Each **Slide** block represents one slide.

## Add Slides Show and a Slide

1. Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize**.
2. Open the page template you want to edit.
3. If the template does not contain the section, click **Add section** and select **Slides Show**.
4. Expand **Slides Show** in the left panel.
5. Click **Add Slide**.
6. Select the new Slide block and configure its image and text.
7. Click **Save**.

Repeat **Add Slide** for additional slides. Every Slide can use a different image, heading, description, and link.

## Manage multiple Slides

- Select a Slide block to edit it.
- Drag blocks to change their order.
- Click **Add Slide** to create another.
- Use **Remove block** to delete the selected slide.

## Slide Image

Choose the background or hero image for the slide. Use clear images with consistent proportions and check responsive cropping on desktop and mobile.

## Headline

- **Heading Position** places the primary heading within the slide.
- **Headline** sets the main title and supports basic rich-text formatting.

Keep the title concise and ensure strong contrast with the image.

## Caption

- **Caption Position** places the supporting copy.
- **Year & Copyright** adds a year, copyright, or short brand label such as `2026©`.
- **Description** adds supporting or brand copy.

Caption content is secondary and should remain brief.

## Call to Action

- **Call to Action Text** sets the action label, such as `View new products` or `Shop now`.
- **Link URL** selects the product, collection, page, or other destination.
- **Call to action style** displays the action as a **Link** or **Button**.

::: tip Recommendation
Use an action label that clearly describes the destination, and verify the Link URL.
:::

## Meta

Meta displays a short additional label such as `AI`, `New`, or a campaign note.

- **Meta Position** sets its location.
- **Meta Text** sets the short label.

Leave Meta Text blank when it is not needed.

## Before saving

- Confirm images are clear and correctly cropped.
- Keep Headline, Caption, and Meta from overlapping.
- Ensure all copy remains readable over the image.
- Test every action link.
- Review desktop and mobile layouts.
- Keep image ratios and writing style consistent across all slides.
