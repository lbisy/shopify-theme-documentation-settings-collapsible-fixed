# Routine Showcase

The **Routine Showcase** places collection products, a content card, and a rotating badge over a large background. Use it for a launch, themed collection, use case, or brand proposition.

![Routine Showcase settings and storefront preview](/images/en/routine-showcase.svg)

## Add the section

Open a template, click **Add section > Routine Showcase**, choose the background and collection, add content and a link, position each visual element, then click **Save**.

## Content and resources

- **Color Scheme**
- **Heading size**
- **Background Image**
- **Select Collection**
- **Eyebrow**
- **Heading**
- **Description**
- **Call to Action Text** and **Link URL**
- **Rotate Badge Text** and **Rotate Badge Percent**

Use a clear landscape background with safe areas for products, text, and badge. Confirm selected products are active, published, included in the collection, properly sorted, and have complete media.

## Positioning

- **Product position**: Left, Center, or Right.
- **Content position**: a combined vertical/horizontal position such as Center right.
- **Badge position**: for example, Bottom right.

Treat the three controls as one composition. Keep the product separate from the content card, prevent the badge from covering the main action, and retest every placement on mobile where space is limited.

Keep Eyebrow short, make the Heading express the central benefit, and use brief paragraphs or lists in Description. Complete both CTA fields. Badge percentages and certifications are display text, not automatically calculated data, so they must be true and supportable.

## Before publishing

Verify background quality, rights, crop, and text contrast; collection publication and order; every position combination on desktop and mobile; headings, description, CTA, and destination; badge layout and claims; responsive overlap, spacing, focus, and Custom CSS. Save and complete the product and link journey on the storefront.
