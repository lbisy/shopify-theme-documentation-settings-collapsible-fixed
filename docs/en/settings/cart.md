# Cart

The **Cart** setting determines how customers view their cart. It affects the store-wide cart entry point and the main experience after an item is added.

## Open the Cart settings

Go to **Shopify admin > Online Store > Themes**, click **Customize**, then open **Theme settings > Cart**.

![Cart settings](/images/en/settings/cart.svg)

## Cart type

### Drawer

The cart opens as a panel over the current page, allowing customers to review or edit it without leaving the product or collection they are viewing.

- Supports a quick add-and-review flow.
- Encourages customers to continue shopping.
- Usually opens from the side on desktop and adapts to narrow mobile screens.

Use **Drawer** when purchase decisions are quick or customers commonly add several products in succession.

### Page

The customer is taken to a dedicated cart page.

- Provides more room for products, quantity controls, and order information.
- Works well for larger or more complex carts.
- Gives customers a clear page they can refresh or return to.
- Uses a full-page layout on desktop and mobile.

Use **Page** when customers need to review detailed information before checkout.

## How to choose

- To let customers add quickly and keep browsing, choose **Drawer**.
- To focus attention on reviewing the cart, choose **Page**.
- Prefer **Drawer** for simple purchases and smaller orders.
- Prefer **Page** when cart information needs more space.

Changing the cart type does not alter cart data; it only changes how the cart is displayed and accessed.

::: info Note
Dynamic checkout buttons such as **Buy now** can go directly to checkout. Cart type primarily affects regular **Add to cart** actions and the cart icon.
:::

## Save and test

Click **Save**, then test desktop and mobile: add a product, open the cart icon, change quantity, remove an item, proceed to checkout, return to the store, and add another item. Confirm prices, quantities, and the checkout button remain fully visible.
