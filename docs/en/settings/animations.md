# Animations

The **Animations** settings control how page content and media appear when they enter the viewport. Thoughtful motion can guide visitors through a page, while excessive or fast animation can become distracting.

## Open the Animations settings

1. In Shopify admin, go to **Online Store > Themes**.
2. Find the current theme and click **Customize**.
3. Open **Theme settings > Animations**.

![Animations settings](/images/en/settings/animations.svg)

## Enable motion

**Enable motion** is the master switch for site-wide animation. When enabled, content and media use the reveal styles below. When disabled, every element appears immediately; no content is hidden.

Preview image-heavy or complex pages on desktop and mobile to make sure scrolling remains smooth.

## Content reveal style

- **None**: display headings, text, buttons, and product information immediately.
- **Fade**: reveal content gradually by changing opacity.
- **Rise**: fade content in while moving it slightly upward.

**Fade** works well on text-heavy pages. Choose **Rise** for a more dynamic homepage or brand presentation.

## Media reveal style

- **None**: display images, video, and other media immediately.
- **Fade**: gradually reveal media.
- **Zoom**: fade media in with a subtle scale effect.

::: warning Note
Using **Zoom** on many elements can distract visitors. Check product images, slideshows, and large banners carefully.
:::

## Motion speed

- **Slow**: a relaxed effect suited to atmospheric pages.
- **Normal**: a balanced choice for most stores.
- **Fast**: a responsive feel for content-dense pages.

The selected speed applies to both content and media. Scroll through several sections to confirm that the pacing feels natural.

## Reveal behavior

- **Once**: each element animates only the first time it enters the viewport.
- **Always**: the animation repeats every time the element re-enters the viewport.

Use **Once** for product grids, blogs, and long pages. **Always** can suit short pages where repeated motion is intentional.

::: info Reduced motion
If a visitor enables the operating system's reduced-motion preference, or motion is disabled in the theme, content appears immediately.
:::

## Recommended combinations

- **Clean and quick**: Content **Fade**, Media **Fade**, **Fast**, **Once**.
- **Natural and balanced**: Content **Rise**, Media **Fade**, **Normal**, **Once**.
- **Atmospheric**: Content **Rise**, Media **Zoom**, **Slow**, **Once**.
- **No animation**: disable **Enable motion**, or set both reveal styles to **None**.

## Save and test

Click **Save**, then check the homepage, collection pages, and product pages. Confirm initial content appears promptly, text and images reveal smoothly, product cards do not stutter, mobile scrolling remains responsive, and reduced-motion mode makes all content immediately visible.
